About Bhagalpur

Bhagalpur is a city in the state of Bihar, India. It is famous for its silk industry and is often referred to as the Silk City. The city has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its historical monuments and temples.

Places to Visit

  • Kahalgaon
  • Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary
  • Budhanath Temple
  • Mandar Parvat
